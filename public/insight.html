Get a Second Opinion on Your Business Accounts — Before They’re Final

Helping UK business owners gain clarity and confidence through an independent financial review of draft accounts.

Running a small or medium-sized business is demanding — and reviewing your accounts shouldn't add to the stress. Clarity Check is a specialist accounting review service offering business owners a fresh, unbiased perspective on their financial statements — without interfering with your relationship with your existing accountant.

We provide a second opinion on your draft accounts, focusing on clarity, accuracy, and insight. Using the draft accounts later available via Companies House and a comparative trial balance (current vs. prior year), we deliver an independent, expert review — no access to internal systems or sensitive data required.

Our simple, secure, and non-intrusive process draws on years of experience preparing and reviewing accounts. You’ll receive key financial observations and thoughtful, strategic questions you can raise with your accountant — empowering you to make better-informed decisions.

With years of hands-on experience preparing and reviewing accounts, we provide expert analysis that:

  • Highlights key financial observations
  • Raises important questions to verify business accounts
  • Helps you finalise your accounts work with informed insights

Whether you’re unsure about your current numbers or just want peace of mind, Clarity Check helps you bridge the gap between accountants and business owners with practical insights and financial transparency — so you can ask better questions, make smarter decisions, and take control of your financial understanding.
